About Product

The Purpose -


Have you ever gone in your pool early Spring or Fall and hopped right back out because of how freezing the water was. Well, with Solar Blanket Covers, this will never be an issue since it will effortlessly heat your pool simply from the suns energy. With our revolutionary technology, your pool will be warm and be energy efficient in the process.

Specifications

Employing a solar pool cover will assist in heating pool water and help conserve money on utility bills. Solar blankets are commonly available in a rectangular form factor for both above and in-ground pools. Solar pool covers are extremely powerful that can raise your water temperature by as much as 15 degrees with the power of the sun. It absorbs and traps the energy of the sun without using a gas heater or propane pool heater, therefore allowing you to save 50%-70% on your heating bill. Therefore, you not only will be saving money but, helping the envirement by using this green energy heating form factor. A solar cover maintains the sun’s warmth in the water during the evening when it starts to get colder as the summer season fades. By preserving the pool water warm, you’re spreading the enjoyment you can achieve in the pool for the season. This is functional in the spring when the day starts to get mild but isn’t too warm. The solar cover can kick start the heating process and get you in the pool early.

Usability

Solar pool covers are remarkably similar to very strong bubble wraps that sit on the top of the pool. It connects to the pool as surface pressure, and its weight holds onto the exterior. One side includes the bubbles while the other side holds a strong flat layer. The bubbles assist as an insulator to prevent heat transfer out of the pool.

• It hovers on the surface of the pool and allows the sun's rays to enter the pool to heat the water.
• It serves as a massive blanket and insulates the pool increasing heat preservation, especially during the evening and during the breezy climate.
• No water is exposed to the air, evaporation is greatly reduced. As a result, you continually won't be needing to add more water.
